Thanks for stopping by. We've opened this section of the site as our
regular home for clinic updates — the kind of news that doesn't fit
neatly on the services or facilities pages, but that our patients and
their families often ask about.

## What you'll find here

Over the coming months we plan to share:

- **Community health camps** — free diabetes, blood pressure, and BMI
  screening events we host with local partners.
- **Awareness drives** — talks and campaigns around World Diabetes Day,
  cardiovascular health month, and other public-health milestones.
- **Patient education seminars** — evening sessions we run for people
  living with diabetes, hypertension, or thyroid conditions.
- **Clinic announcements** — schedule changes, new facilities, or
  reminders about seasonal illnesses in Podanur.

Each post will include photos from the day and a short write-up so
you can see what we've been up to even if you couldn't attend.

## Follow along

The best way to hear about upcoming camps and seminars is to
[message us on WhatsApp](https://wa.me/919865244531). We keep a small
broadcast list — no group chatter, just event announcements once or
twice a month.

If you'd rather visit in person, our regular consultation hours are
5:30 PM to 9:00 PM on working days, at SIFA Tower on Podanur Main
Road near Karuparayan Kovil bus stop.

We'll see you at the next one.
